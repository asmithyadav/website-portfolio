Who We Are

Travel isn't a luxury —
it's a perspective

Founded by a group of seasoned backpackers in 2013, BackpackWithUs was born from a simple belief: the best journeys happen when you travel light, move slow, and connect deeply with the world around you.

We're not a tour operator in the traditional sense. We're your fellow traveler — planning every detail so you're free to be fully present in every moment.

🌍

Sustainable Travel

Low-impact itineraries that give back to local communities.

👥

Small Groups

Max 12 travelers per trip for a genuine, personal experience.

🗺

Expert Guides

Local guides who live and breathe the places they show you.

♻️

Carbon Neutral
